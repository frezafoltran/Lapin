This algorithm could be used to efficiently store the Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) of small audio samples. FFTs is a mathematical tool to retrieve the frequency components of an audio sample. Consider storing a simplified FFT of an audio sample in an array such that each index of the array corresponds to the FFT value at a point in time.
